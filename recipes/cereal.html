Cereal

Description

This is your go to morning breakfast meal when you are in a rush or just lazy like myself. I highly reccomend staying away from sugary cereals.

Ingredients

  • favorite cereal
  • milk
  • almond milk alternative to milk

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. pour cereal into bowl
  3. pour milk into bowl
  4. eat with spoon ensuring you have both milk and cereal with each bite
